Privacy Policy for MyQA

Last updated: 6/10/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MyQA ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our label verification application ("the App").

2. Information We Collect

Our app collects the following information:

  • Camera Data: We require camera access to scan and verify product labels. Images are processed on your device.
  • Label Information: We collect and store information from scanned labels including APN numbers, dates, and weights.
  • Device Information: Basic device information required for app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Verifying product labels
  • Generating verification reports
  • Creating CSV exports of verified items
  • Improving app functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers. CSV exports are controlled entirely by you.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Any data export (such as CSV files) is initiated and controlled by you.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the app's interface
  • Export your data in CSV format
  • Delete your data using the app's clear function

7. Camera Usage

Our app requires camera access to function. We use the camera only for scanning product labels. Camera data is processed in real-time and is not stored unless you explicitly save a verification result.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or need any support please contact us at MyQa2025scan@outlook.com .
